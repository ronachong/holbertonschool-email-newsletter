Reef

A Platform for the Tech Community

Hi Friend!

We are a new micro-blogging platform in the works by students of the Holberton School of Engineering.

Here at Holberton, building a thriving tech community--inside and outside the school--is a major part of our philosophy. We like to stay connected and bounce off each others' energy. And while face-to-face is awesome, we feel that also having our own micro-blogging platform will allow for even greater access to each others' ideas and thoughts.

While Reef could be constrained to only Hippokampoi students and mentors, we decided that we wanted Reef to be for the broader tech community. If you're interested in joining in, feel free to check us out!
